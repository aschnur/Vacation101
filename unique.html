Unique Things To Do

  • Food Lovers: NYC Pizza Tour

    • For the food lovers, the NYC Pizza tour is the way to go. This walking tour of greenwich village and the lower west side of NYC features visits to three different famous NYC pizzerias as well as a trip to a warehouse where industrial ovens used for making pizza are bulilt. The tour is led by a mad who is thoroughly obssesed with pizza to the point where he wrote a book about the different types of pizza boxes. If you like pizza in any way, shape, or form and want to do something a little more unique with your time in NYC, check this tour out, it will not dissapoint!

  • Art Lovers: MOMA, MET, Guggenheim

    • NYC offers several of the most famous art museums in the United States and in the World. Make sure to check out at least one of these museums if you are an art lover in NYC as the art collections in these museums are a can't miss highlight for many. If you can go to all three, make sure to do so, but if not, choose the one that best suits you. Keep in mind that the museums all feature different types of art, with MOMA especially focused on Modern Art.

  • Sports Lovers: Knicks/Rangers Game at MSG

    • If you love sports, make sure to take the time to check of the most famous arena in the world: Madison Square Garden. Whether it means going to a NY Rangers game, a NY Knicks game, or even seeing a boxing match, this world class sports arena is a must for sports fans visiting in NYC.

  • Political Nuts: Visit the United Nations NYC Headquarters

    • Do you love politics? Have you ever taken an interest in the UN? If so, make sure to make time for a visit to the United Nations, which houses one of its several headquarters in NYC. Make sure to schedule a tour that includes a visit to the famous room of the UN General Assembly, as you can marvel at the room where all the action and deliberation takes place between the world's leaders and their UN representatives.
